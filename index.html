Why Caitlyn Jungle?

"Looks my stupid jungler locked in an absolute braindead pick". I am not a troll and will explain the pick to you. Keep in mind I am still new and learning jungle so do not blame the pick but blame me instead. Her clear speed is very good for an adc at 3:20 with 70% hp. Just think of her like female Graves support jungler. It is something you have to experience and try. Also If I make it to the Exodia build you guys get a free win (unless they have mountain soul)! Please do not be that guy that complains from the time someone locks it in till the end of the game, just focus and try your best!

Pros

  • Amazing Mid/Late Game
  • Easy To Catchup If Behind
  • Good Objective Control
  • Great Ganks If Laners Have CC

Cons

  • Terrible Ganks if Laners Have No CC
  • Horrible Early Game
  • Weak To Invades If No Setup
  • Really Really Squishy

Builds and Runes

Here are the builds and runes if you want to play Caitlyn Jungle for youself! You can change up the build however you want, I like to get a lot of movespeed. If you win feats make sure you pick up the tier 3 boots of lucidity as they are really good as the 8% movespeed gain when you use an ability might actually be better than swiftness boots.
