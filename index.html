Pros

  • Amazing Mid/Late Game
  • Easy To Catchup If Behind
  • Good Objective Control
  • Great Ganks If Laners Have CC

Cons

  • Terrible Ganks if Laners Have No CC
  • Horrible Early Game
  • Weak To Invades If No Setup
  • Really Really Squishy

Builds and Runes

Here are the builds and runes if you want to play Caitlyn Jungle for youself! You can change up the build however you want, I like to get a lot of movespeed. If you win feats make sure you pick up the tier 3 boots of lucidity as they are really good as the 8% movespeed gain when you use an ability might actually be better than swiftness boots.

Situational Items

RFC gives you extra range if you have trouble hitting the enemy. Force of Nature is built when the enemy team is all AP and it gives you 4% movespeed, it really is not worth building even if they have 1AD. Opportunity is more damage. Dead Man's Plate isn't the best item, its not going to save your life, but the extra movespeed and damage is nice to have especially if their team has a lot of slows. Maw is pretty good if they have someone that is AP going to one shot you like a Leblanc and you need it to survive. Serpants fang is good if the enemy has a lot of shields like Shieldbow, Lulu shield, mountain soul, Sett, barrier etc. Blood thirster is good if the enemy team doesn't have too much burst and you can leech off their tanks to heal up. Deaths Dance, just buy bloodthirster instead since this item is not good on ranged champs. As for GA I feel like it is a trash item.
